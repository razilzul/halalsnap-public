HalalSnap Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

1. Introduction

HalalSnap ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Camera Access

We request camera permission to allow you to scan product ingredients and packaging for halal verification analysis. Photos are processed locally on your device and sent securely to our analysis service.

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app, including:

  • Number of scans performed
  • App usage frequency
  • Feature usage patterns

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Product Analysis: Camera images are used solely for halal ingredient analysis
  • Service Improvement: Usage data helps us enhance the app experience
  • Notifications
